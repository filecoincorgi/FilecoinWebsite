About

What is the Filecoin Foundation?

The Filecoin Foundation (FF) is an independent organization focused on the coordination, assistance, and support services in connection with the development of open source software and open protocols for decentralized data storage and retrieval networks.

What is Filecoin?

The Filecoin Foundation (FF) is an independent organization focused on the coordination, assistance, and support services in connection with the development of open source software and open protocols for decentralized data storage and retrieval networks.

How is the Filecoin Foundation related to Filecoin?

Filecoin is not controlled by anyone - it is built and maintained by thousands of people, companies, organizations and users all over the world to store humanity’s most important information. The FF does not control Filecoin, nor is it the only organization that funds critical development of Filecoin-related technologies. The foundation is part of a large ecosystem of organizations, individuals, and companies that support Filecoin.

The Filecoin Foundation’s role is to allocate resources to critical projects to promote decentralized data storage, and to be a valued voice within the Filecoin ecosystem in advocating for Filecoin to the outside world.

This includes:

  • • Accelerating ongoing development so that Filecoin can more easily make the web more decentralized, efficient, and robust across many use cases.
  • •PromotingTo promote widespread adoption of Filecoin, through community development,policy, documentation, education, and tooling, so that the network meaningfully Filecoin actually improves the web at scale.
