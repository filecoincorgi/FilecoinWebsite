About

What is the Filecoin Foundation?

The Filecoin Foundation (FF) is an independent organization focused on the coordination, assistance, and support services in connection with the development of open source software and open protocols for decentralized data storage and retrieval networks.

What is Filecoin?

Filecoin is a peer-to-peer network that stores files, with built-in economic incentives to ensure files are stored reliably over time. The mission of Filecoin is to create a decentralized, efficient, and robust foundation for humanityâ€™s information.

How is the Filecoin Foundation related to Filecoin?

Filecoin is not controlled by anyone; it is a decentralized network built and maintained by thousands of people and organizations all over the world. The Filecoin Foundation facilitates governance of the Filecoin network, funds critical development projects, supports the growth of the Filecoin ecosystem, and advocates for Filecoin and the Decentralized Web.
